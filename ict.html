Elon Musk

My life

Elon Musk, born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa, is a highly influential entrepreneur and innovator. Raised in a family with a strong interest in technology, Musk developed an early passion for computing and science fiction. He left South Africa to attend the University of Pretoria and later transferred to the University of Pennsylvania.

About Me

Musk is the founder, chairman, CEO of SpaceX; angel investor, CEO, product architect of Tesla;Owner, chairman and CTO of X Corp.;
Founder of the Boring Company and xAI; Co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI; and president of the Musk Foundation.
He is the wealthiest person in the world, with an estimated net worth of $billion according to Forbes, primarily from his ownership stakes in Tesla and SpaceX.

X.com and Paypal

  • Musk co-founded Zip2, a web software company, in 1996, which he later sold.
  • He then went on to create X.com, an online payment company, which eventually became PayPal after a merger.
  • PayPal was acquired by eBay in 2002.
Space X
  1. Founded in 2002
  2. Focuses on reducing space transportation costs
  3. Developed Falcon and Starship rockets
  4. Successful launch of the first crewed commercial spaceflight in 2020
  5. Long-term goal: Establishing a human settlement on Mars
